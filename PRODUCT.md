# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Primary audience: Hiring managers and recruiters looking for high-quality full-time technical talent. Secondary audience: Fellow developers and potential collaborators.

## Product Purpose
A professional personal portfolio to showcase technical proficiency, academic background, and project experience. The goal is to establish credibility and secure a full-time software engineering position by demonstrating both "proof of work" and a refined design sensibility.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Clean, Immersive, Intuitive. It should feel like a premium tech product—stable and authoritative, yet forward-thinking and technically sophisticated. Inspired by high-contrast dark modes, crisp typography, and intentional use of 3D space.

## Anti-references
- **Excessive Flashiness**: No over-the-top, distracting animations that hinder navigation.
- **Boxy Scaffolding**: Avoid rigid, old-school grid layouts that feel like a generic template.
- **Heavy Shadows/Generic Gradients**: Avoid the "2018 SaaS" look of deep blurs and rainbow gradients.
- **Boring/Flat**: Should not feel like a static résumé; it needs a "living" technical pulse.

## Design Principles
1. **Trust through Proof**: Use badges, certifications, and activity metrics (like GitHub activity) to provide immediate, verifiable credibility.
2. **Subtle Depth**: Leverage the 3D capabilities (Three.js) for immersive backgrounds or interactive elements that add "texture" without clutter.
3. **Information Hierarchy**: Prioritize readability and navigation. Recruiters should find the CV, projects, and contact info in under 10 seconds.
4. **Intuitive Interactivity**: Every interactive element should provide subtle, elegant feedback (glows, micro-transitions) to feel "alive."

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Responsive-First**: Must be perfectly fluid from mobile to ultra-wide desktops.
- **High Contrast**: Ensure text is sharp and legible against dark backgrounds (meeting WCAG standards).
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect system settings for users who prefer minimal movement.
