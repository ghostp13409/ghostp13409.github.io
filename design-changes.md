- The Introduction Section is too in consistent and big and boxy and doesn't really do much in terms of providing a good simple engaging but clear introduction about me.

- The Projects still look very messy and just there, they don't tell a story.

- A complete redesign of the website to make it more engaging and tell a story about me.

- **The Introduction Section**: It should be more engaging and tell a story about me, and it should be more compact and consistent with the rest of the website. It should talk about me in a very human and connecting way.
- **The Project Section**: It needs the redesign from the ground up. in terms of both design and content. It should show as a sort of timeline like the education section's college semester. So you can show it as a photo(full image) then on the side of it a text paragraph or a key points about features. then in below than some skill or tech badges. make each one unique and visually appealing.
- From start to bottom, all the content should look consistent and part of the same story.
- I need it to look and sound premium and tailored. Make things compact, clean and consistent.
